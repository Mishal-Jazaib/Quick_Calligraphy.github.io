Arabic Calligraphy

Arabic calligraphy is the artistic practice of handwriting, where the writing is constructed from a modified and often cursive form of the Arabic script.

Styles of Arabic Calligraphy

Thuluth

Thuluth is known for its clarity and readability.

Naskh

Naskh is a more ornate style, often used in formal documents.

Diwani

Diwani is a cursive style, used for official documents.
