Indic Calligraphy

Indic calligraphy refers to the art of beautiful writing in the scripts used by languages of the Indian subcontinent.

Styles of Indic Calligraphy

Vatteluttu

Vatteluttu is known for its elegance and fluidity.

Tirhuta

Tirhuta is a more ornate style, often used in formal documents.

Modi

Modi is a cursive style, used for official documents.
